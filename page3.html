Style Tips

Easy, practical tips to help you elevate your wardrobe and feel confident every day.

1. Build Your Basics

Start with simple pieces like neutral tops, jeans, and a good jacket. They make mixing and matching effortless.

2. Add One Statement Piece

Whether it's bold shoes, a fun bag, or bright jewelry, one standout item instantly elevates your look.

3. Balance Your Outfit

If your top is oversized, go for fitted bottoms. If your pants are loose, choose a more fitted top.

4. Layer Smart

Layering adds depth and style—try a cardigan over a simple tee or a jacket over a dress.

5. Stick to a Color Palette

Choosing 2–3 main colors helps your wardrobe stay consistent and creates cleaner outfits.
