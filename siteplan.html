Simple Style Guide – Site Plan

Site Purpose

The purpose of my website, Simple Style Guide, is to help people find easy and cute outfit ideas without feeling overwhelmed. A lot of people want to look stylish but don’t know where to start or feel stressed when trying to choose what to wear. My site keeps things simple by giving clear outfit ideas, basic style tips, and inspiration that anyone can use. I want visitors to feel confident and enjoy putting outfits together instead of worrying about it. The site is built to be friendly, clean, and easy to follow, so every person — no matter their style level — can find something helpful.

Target Audience

The site is for teens, young adults, and anyone who wants simple fashion advice they can use every day. This includes people who want casual everyday outfits, students wanting quick ideas, and anyone who likes cute styles without needing expensive brands. The audience prefers short tips, clear images, and an aesthetic look that is modern and easy to understand.

Home Page Content

The home page introduces the purpose of the website: helping people find simple and stylish outfit ideas. It gives visitors a quick overview of what they can find on the site, and it uses clean images and icons to make the page feel welcoming. The text explains that fashion doesn’t need to be complicated and that small ideas can make your outfits look better right away.

Images: Logo, hero image, 3 category icons

Outfit Ideas – Page 2

The Outfit Ideas page shows everyday looks that are cute, simple, and easy to copy. Each outfit card has an image and a short explanation about where you could wear it and how to style it. The page includes matching photos that pair well together visually. The tips focus on real-life outfits that are comfortable and fun, like weekend looks, school outfits, and basic casual styles.

Images: 2ndstreet.png, clothes-2.webp

Style Tips – Page 3

The Style Tips page gives simple advice on how to build better outfits. This includes choosing colors that go together, adding accessories, picking the right layers, and understanding how to balance loose and fitted clothing. The goal is to help visitors learn small tricks that make a big difference in their style. The page uses simple icons and example images.

Images: tips-1.jpg, tips-2.jpg

Overall Goal

The overall goal of the website is to make fashion feel easy. I want people to come to the site and quickly find inspiration they can use right away. The site is simple, clean, and focused on helping people feel confident in their clothing and find outfits they can actually wear in their daily lives.
